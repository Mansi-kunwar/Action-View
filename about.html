Our Inception

"MeraMonitor," the brainchild of AAPNA Infotech, was born out of the necessity imposed by the COVID-19 pandemic. With the world shifting towards remote work, our visionary CEO, Abhijit Roy, recognized the need for a tool to ensure the productivity and efficiency of remote teams.

After evaluating existing time tracking software, which often proved costly and fell short of our expectations, our skilled IT Commando team embarked on a journey to develop a superior application.

Today, we proudly announce that "MeraMonitor" is LIVE. This AI-driven tool, shaped by the dedication of over 200 Commandos and valued partners, surpasses all competition. Its name, "MeraMonitor," reflects its dual commitment to enhancing both employee and organizational productivity.

As we continue to navigate the evolving landscape of work, "MeraMonitor" stands as a testament to innovation and adaptability.
